Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Investra?

Investra is a modern stock dashboard that lets you search, track, compare, and analyze stock market data interactively.

2. Is the stock data real-time?

Currently, we use periodically updated offline datasets. Live data integration is planned in future releases.

3. What is a stock watchlist?

A watchlist lets you save and monitor your favorite stocks in one place for easy access and analysis.

4. How can I add stocks to my watchlist?

From the homepage or search panel, simply click on the "+" button to save the stock to your personalized watchlist.

5. What are 'Top Movers'?

'Top Movers' are stocks that have shown major changes in price — they’re rising or falling rapidly in the market.

6. Can I compare stocks side-by-side?

Yes! On the stock detail page, use the “Compare With” section to compare multiple stocks' key metrics visually.

7. What types of stocks are available on Investra?

We feature Indian and global stocks from major indices for diversified market analysis.

8. Can I filter stocks by performance?

Absolutely. Use the filters to sort stocks by metrics like market cap, volume, price change, and more.

9. How does dark mode work?

Click on your profile icon and select “Toggle Dark Mode” — your preference is saved for future visits.

10. Is Investra mobile-friendly?

Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works well across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

11. Can I see historical performance?

Yes. Navigate to the stock’s detail page to view interactive charts with historical trends and movements.

12. Will Investra provide investment advice?

No, Investra is a tool for information and analysis. Always consult a financial advisor before investing.

13. Can I export stock data?

Not yet, but we are working on features that will let users download data or export charts soon.

14. Is Investra free to use?

Yes! Investra is 100% free for all users. Our mission is to make market analysis accessible to everyone.

15. Will there be a mobile app?

Yes! We’re planning to launch an Android and iOS app soon. Stay tuned!
